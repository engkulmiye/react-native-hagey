# 📱 Subscription Tracker

A modern React Native application designed to help users track, manage, and optimize their recurring digital subscriptions and bills.

## 🚀 Features

- **Centralized Dashboard**: View your total monthly and annual subscription costs at a glance.
- **Renewal Alerts**: Receive push notifications before a subscription renews or a free trial ends.
- **Calendar Integration**: A visual calendar showing all upcoming payment dates.
- **Custom Categories**: Organize your spending by Entertainment, Work, Health, and more.
- **Spending Analytics**: Beautiful charts to help you identify where your money is going.
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Track subscriptions in different currencies with automatic conversion.
- **Privacy First**: All data is stored locally on your device.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework**: React Native (Expo)
- **Styling**: NativeWind (Tailwind CSS)
- **Navigation**: Expo Router
